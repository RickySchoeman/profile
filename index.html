A Mobile App for the Conscious Consumer

My idea behind Amandla was that while most people would like to be better social and environmental consumers, there are so many different metrics to measure this by - which can be overwhelming. Amandla makes this easier, by allowing a user to filter the desired social and environmental causes they would like to support, and directs them to businesses that are aligned with these beliefs

Amandla was built over 2 weeks, by my team and I, using Ruby on Rails, HTML + CSS, and JavaScript. Built for mobile
